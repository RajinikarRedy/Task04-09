Brakes are the most important safety parts in the vehicles. Generally, all of the vehicles have their own safety devices to stop their car. Function of brakes to slow and stop the rotation of the wheel. To stop the wheel, braking pads are forced mechanically against rotor disc on both surfaces. They compulsory for all of the modern vehicles and the safe operation of vehicles. Brakes transform the kinetic energy of the car into heat energy.
